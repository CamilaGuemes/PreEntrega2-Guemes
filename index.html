EMPA - TEAM
Building Connected Teams

Helping teams grow by creating meaningful connections and making collaboration easy for everyone.

¿Why EMPA-TEAM?

Empa-Team Builders is a collaborative project that emerged between Gotya (a foundation dedicated to wellness and mental health services) and Dance Is Work (an organization focused on recreational activities and artistic management). Both organizations aim to promote well-being and health, contributing to the development of a fulfilling life through the use of various interactive tools. These organizations have come together in this project to combine resources and strive to create enriching experiences. As a result of this interdisciplinary work, we have utilized tools from group dynamics and human capital management (drawing on psychological knowledge) to create workshops aimed at work teams, with the goal of strengthening interpersonal bonds through team-building dynamics that help organizations.
