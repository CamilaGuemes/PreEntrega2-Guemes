Our Story

Empa-team builders is born from the fusion of empathy and teamwork, with the mission of creating connections where people can feel part of something greater: a sense of belonging and teamwork.

We believe that teamwork is not just about achieving goals, but about doing it in a way that strengthens the cohesion among people.

Dance is Work

At Dance is Work, we develop innovative projects in the performing arts, focusing on the connection with the body and the interconnection with others. It is a tool for self-discovery and well-being that fosters commitment to our goals.

GOTYA

At GOTYA, we offer psychotherapy, psychoeducation workshops, and artistic activities to combat stress and strengthen mental health. We aim to create a positive impact in the community by fostering a well-being environment.
